Network Analysis

  • [Jun 2019] Networks can reveal interesting insights. Network analysis is gaining traction in people analytics. Observing communication pattern within an organization through email network, social media etc. It can reveal interesting informaion about organization structure, efficiency, team dynamics and productivity etc. In the following report Analyzing and Mitigating Phishing Outbreaks: a Case Study on the 2016 DNC Email Network we use temporal information extracted from email network to detect social hierarchy and groups within an organization.
