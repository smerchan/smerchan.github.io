Network Analysis

  • [Jun 2019] Network Analytics can be used to understand Social Dynamics within an Organization. Email and social network communication pattern can reveal interesting informaion about the organization structure, its efficiency, team dynamics and productivity. In the following report Analyzing and Mitigating Phishing Outbreaks: a Case Study on the 2016 DNC Email Network we used temporal information from a corporate email network to detect social hierarchy and social groups within an organization.
